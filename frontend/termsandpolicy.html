Terms & Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CalmWell, a platform designed to support youth mental health through advisor connections and AI-powered tools. By using our platform, you agree to the terms below and acknowledge our approach to privacy and data handling.

2. Terms of Use

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use CalmWell.
  • You agree not to misuse the platform for illegal or harmful activities.
  • Advisors are expected to behave professionally and respectfully with users.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

3. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Any data collected is used only to improve your experience and is never sold to third parties.

  • We store user data (such as messages and profiles) securely in our database (MongoDB).
  • We do not collect sensitive personal identifiers like your full name or medical history unless explicitly provided by you.
  • Your chatbot interactions may be processed using third-party AI services like Wolfram for response generation, but we do not store identifiable data with them.

4. Chatbot Disclaimer

The CalmWell chatbot is an AI-powered tool designed for general emotional support and mental well-being tips. It is not a substitute for professional medical or psychological advice.

5. Advisor Guidelines

  • Advisors must be verified and follow ethical communication practices.
  • They should not diagnose or prescribe treatments unless qualified and permitted by law in their region.
  • All advisor-user conversations are expected to remain confidential and professional.

6. Emergency Use

CalmWell is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, please contact your local emergency services or national helplines.

7. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the platform or via email (if provided).

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these terms or your data, please contact us at support@calmwell.org.
